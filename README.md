üìå Key Findings

Here are a few of the main insights I discovered:
	‚Ä¢	Customers with shorter tenure are more likely to churn.
	‚Ä¢	Monthly contracts are strongly correlated with high churn rates.
	‚Ä¢	The ‚ÄúElectronic check‚Äù payment method is linked to higher churn.
	‚Ä¢	Subscribing to additional services (like online security and technical support) is associated with better customer retention.
	‚Ä¢	Surprisingly, fiber optic customers showed higher churn rates‚Äîpossibly pointing to issues with service quality or unmet expectations.

üí° Recommendations

	‚Ä¢	Introduce welcome programs focused on onboarding new customers.
